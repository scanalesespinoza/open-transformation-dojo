What it's DOJO?

DOJO is the mechanism which STP selected to carry out the accompaniment of consultants and architects of Delivery Latam in order to prepare them for Transformational projects, all of the above under the initiative of STP Latam - Incubation Program

The vision of the Dojo today proposes and seeks to contribute internally to Red Hat under the following vision: Open Transformation Dojo (A.K.A. DOJO) Is an Open Source Effort to increase personales relations and experiences across Global IT based on an strong Mentor Mentee aproach and digital transformation need with the demands of the information age
